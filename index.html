About

I am an economist at the International Monetary Fund (IMF). My work focuses on international macroeconomics and emerging markets. I have served on IMF country teams across regions—including Argentina, Bolivia, Burkina Faso, Canada and Pakistan— contributing to research and policy work. I hold a PhD in economics from Toulouse School of Economics, France.
