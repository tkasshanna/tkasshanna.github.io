About

I am an economist at the International Monetary Fund (IMF). My work focuses on international macroeconomics and emerging markets, with applications to monetary policy transmission, inflation dynamics, and labor-market institutions. I have served on IMF country teams across regions—including Argentina, Canada, Bolivia, Pakistan—and contribute to research and policy design for decision makers.

Research Interests

  • International Macroeconomics
  • Fiscal Policy
  • Emerging Markets
